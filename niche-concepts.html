Direction

Strips away ornamentation for a cleaner, more "tool-first" feel. Tabs use underline indicators instead of rounded pills. Cards lose dividers for tighter density. The Space Grotesk lowercase "niche" title with a sage dot reads techy-but-warm — like a modern SaaS product with soul.

Direction

Leans further into the cozy "lo-fi girl" aesthetic. Warmer mid-tones, lavender accents on the sidebar, and colored left-borders on creator cards that alternate sage and lavender. Cormorant Garamond italic "Niche" title reads like a boutique magazine. The "by iamsocial" tagline gives credit with elegance.
